Malaria is caused by Plasmodium via Anopheles mosquitoes.
Symptoms: cyclical fever, chills, sweating, headache, nausea, fatigue.
Warning signs: confusion, breathing difficulty, jaundice, bleeding, seizures.
Diagnosis: rapid diagnostic test or blood smear.
Prevention: insecticide-treated nets, repellents, indoor residual spraying.
Treatment: species-specific antimalarials; seek prompt medical care.
